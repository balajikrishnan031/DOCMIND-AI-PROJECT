5 Core Intelligent Modules

Explore how DocMind AI organizes complex educational materials into actionable learning assets:

1. Semantic Syllabus Mapping

Upload unit-wise course syllabi (B.E. Sem 1-8, GATE, Group Exams). DocMind AI parses topics and semantically maps each chapter topic to exact textbook pages.

2. 3D Recall Flashcards

Active recall study decks featuring 3D card flips. Automatically synthesizes key terms, formulas, and definitions from textbooks for instant revision.

3. Practice Exam Quizzes

Generates targeted multiple-choice question tests with detailed solution feedback and automated scoreboards to log assessment metrics over time.

4. Concept Mind Maps

Interactive Vis.js node network graphics mapping linkages between core definitions, formulas, and keywords in zoomable visual hierarchies.

5. Role & Grade Hub

Tailor access criteria by role (Student, Teacher, Professor, HOD, Dean) and education grade level (Std 1-12, B.E., M.E., Competitive Exams).

Purpose & Educational Impact

Educational textbooks and multi-unit course syllabi are often fragmented and overwhelming. DocMind AI resolves this challenge by acting as an artificial intelligence educational tutor powered by Groq Llama 3.1 70B & local vector embeddings.

Why it was built: To streamline study workflows for B.E. / M.E. engineering students across 8 semesters, competitive exam candidates preparing for GATE and Group Exams, as well as faculty members (Professors, HODs, Deans) who need to evaluate curriculum alignment and generate automated assessment banks.
