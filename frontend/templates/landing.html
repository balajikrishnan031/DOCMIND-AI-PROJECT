FAQ

Your questions answered.

Everything you need to know about DocMind AI. Still have questions? Reach out anytime.

01 What makes DocMind AI different from other tools?
DocMind AI is specifically engineered for multi-tier education (Std 1-12, B.E. Sem 1-8, GATE). It provides 100% page-exact citations, auto-generates 3D flashcards, Bloom's classified quizzes, and syllabus unit mappings directly from course textbooks.
02 Will there be any copyright issues using DocMind AI?
No. DocMind AI is a private educational study tool operating on your locally uploaded textbooks for personal study, research, and revision (Fair Use Educational exemption).
03 How does the Groq AI Llama 3.1 70B integration work?
DocMind AI integrates with Groq's high-speed inference engine (`llama-3.1-70b-versatile`). Groq processes text chunks retrieved via RAG vector search in milliseconds to generate accurate answers and study materials.
04 Does DocMind AI work on Vercel without data loss?
Yes! DocMind AI supports Turso (LibSQL Cloud SQLite edge database). While local development uses `database.db`, Vercel production connects to Turso via `TURSO_DATABASE_URL` so all signups, flashcards, and quiz scores persist safely.
