Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered.

Everything you need to know about DocMind AI's educational features and platform capabilities.

01 How does DocMind AI map syllabi to textbook pages?
DocMind AI's regex syllabus parser identifies Unit I-V boundaries and topic lists. Using cosine vector similarity search over embedded PDF page chunks, it maps every topic directly to its exact page number in the course textbook.
02 What subjects are covered in the Std 1-12 School Portal?
The School Portal covers Mathematics, Environmental Science, General Science, Social Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science from Standard 1 to Standard 12 with step-by-step problem solvers.
03 How does the Groq Llama 3.1 70B AI integration work?
DocMind AI connects to Groq's high-speed inference engine (`llama-3.1-70b-versatile`). Groq evaluates retrieved RAG vector context chunks to synthesize page-exact answers, 3D flashcards, and Bloom's classified quizzes.
04 Can I deploy DocMind AI to Vercel without data loss?
Yes! DocMind AI integrates Turso (LibSQL Cloud SQLite edge database). Production deployments connect to Turso via `TURSO_DATABASE_URL` so all signups, flashcards, and quiz scores persist safely.
