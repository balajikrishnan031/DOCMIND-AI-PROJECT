Bridging the Gap in Document Storage

Students often struggle with managing 100+ PDF note files, lecture presentations, and research articles. Finding specific definitions, formulas, or schedules across disorganized folders becomes a major bottleneck. DocMind AI solves this problem by creating an intelligent, offline-first digital library.

By combining high-performance client-side text extraction (PDF.js) with a dedicated Python NLP backend, DocMind AI automatically indexes the words in your documents, scores sentence importance, extracts key keywords, compiles checklist summaries, and allows you to chat directly with your files. Your files remain completely secure, running locally on your computer with optional cloud AI support.

The 5 Dimensions of DocMind

01

Instant Document Ingestion

Drag and drop multi-page PDF notes. The client extracts and processes text page-by-page in the browser, bypassing server limits and maintaining absolute privacy.

02

Local TF-IDF Modeling

Our Python Flask service calculates term-frequency densities, cleaning out stopwords and sorting primary technical terms to map the core themes of your documents.

03

Key Takeaway Checklist

Automatically scans sentence structures to isolate high-scoring phrases containing major keywords, listing key formulas, dates, or principles as a checklist.

04

Cosine Similarity Chatbot

Consult your files directly. The overlap similarity engine matches your questions against sentence vectors to highlight context passages and provide answers.

05

Glassmorphic File Cabinet

File documents into color-coded folders and searchable tags. Fast metadata indexing lets you look up documents, categories, and keyword occurrences instantly.

Balaji & Team

DocMind AI was developed by Balaji & team of AI systems engineers to simplify document research. We believe information shouldn't be trapped in giant, unsearchable files; it should be interactive, private, and accessible instantly.

Local Tech Stack

Built using vanilla web components, IndexedDB browser storage, and a pure Python Flask backend server. All TF-IDF calculation models and text overlap engines run offline on your machine, ensuring 100% data security.

Design Philosophy

Designed with glassmorphic cards and a fixed wallpaper backdrop (`download (5).jpg`) using sea teal and sand gold gradients. Optimized for eye-pleasing study and research sessions.
